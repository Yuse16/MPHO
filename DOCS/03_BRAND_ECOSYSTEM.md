# 03_BRAND_ECOSYSTEM.md

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the official brand architecture for MPHO.

It explains:

- The role of the master brand.
- The meaning and function of each sub-brand.
- Naming rules.
- Tone of voice.
- Brand relationships.
- Approved usage.
- Prohibited usage.
- Product-language guidelines.

The purpose is to prevent brand confusion as the platform grows.

---

## 2. Master brand

# MPHO

MPHO is the official master brand.

It represents the complete gifting platform, including:

- Customer experience.
- Marketplace.
- Partner network.
- Order coordination.
- Payment coordination.
- Preparation.
- Delivery.
- Tracking.
- Future services.

MPHO is the name customers should remember.

The intended brand meaning is associated with the idea of a gift, a meaningful offering, and something given to another person.

The brand must not be written as:

- MphoApp.
- Mpho Shop.
- MPHO Marketplace.
- MPHO Delivery.

Those may describe functions in copy, but they are not the official master-brand name.

## 5. HADIA

### Role

HADIA is the AI-powered gifting assistant inside MPHO.

### Purpose

HADIA helps the customer find an appropriate gift based on:

- Recipient.
- Occasion.
- Budget.
- Preferences.
- Date.
- Zone.
- Delivery urgency.

### Positioning

Preferred introduction:

> HADIA, tu asistente para encontrar el regalo adecuado.

Approved calls to action:

- Pregúntale a HADIA.
- Encuentra el regalo con HADIA.
- Cuéntale a HADIA para quién es.
- No sé qué regalar.
- Ayúdame a elegir.

### Restrictions

HADIA is not:

- The main company name.
- A separate marketplace.
- A payment agent.
- A courier.
- A promise of availability.
- A human support representative.

### Interface usage

Use `HADIA` in uppercase when referring to the product personality.

Do not write:

- Hadia AI Bot.
- MPHO AI.
- Chat MPHO.
- Hadiia.
- HADI-A.

---

## 6. MPHORA

### Role

MPHORA is the fast-delivery mode inside MPHO.

### Name construction

The name combines the MPHO master brand with the idea of “ahora”.

Visual emphasis may be used in marketing:

```text
MPHOra
MPHO + ahora
```

The official written name remains:

# MPHORA

### Purpose

MPHORA identifies gifts that can be prepared and delivered quickly after operational validation.

### Positioning

Preferred message:

> MPHORA: opciones disponibles cerca para entregar más rápido.

Approved calls to action:

- Lo necesito MPHORA.
- Ver opciones MPHORA.
- Entrega rápida disponible.
- Recíbelo hoy, sujeto a disponibilidad.
- Tu regalo, ahora.

### Restrictions

MPHORA must not be used when:

- Stock is not confirmed.
- Partner capacity is unknown.
- Delivery coverage is not confirmed.
- The cutoff time has passed.
- The item is by order.
- External delivery timing is uncertain.

Do not use MPHORA as a generic synonym for same-day delivery.

## 8. Punto MPHO

### Role

A Punto MPHO is an approved physical partner location.

A Punto MPHO may:

- Sell products.
- Receive packages.
- Inspect items.
- Wrap.
- Personalize.
- Prepare.
- Store temporarily.
- Dispatch to a courier.
- Deliver locally if approved.

### Positioning

Preferred external message:

> Este negocio forma parte de la red Punto MPHO.

Preferred operational message:

> Punto MPHO asignado.

### Restrictions

Not every registered business is automatically a Punto MPHO.

The status requires approval and defined capabilities.

Do not use the term for:

- Unverified businesses.
- Temporary couriers.
- Customers.
- External marketplace sellers.
- Warehouses not participating in the network.

## 11. Brand relationship rules

### MPHO is always the master brand

Correct:

- HADIA by MPHO.
- MPHORA en MPHO.
- MPHO Aliados.
- Punto MPHO.

Incorrect:

- HADIA Marketplace.
- MPHORA Company.
- Punto HADIA.
- MPHO by HADIA.

### Sub-brands must have one clear function

- HADIA chooses.
- MPHORA accelerates.
- MPHO Aliados operates.
- Punto MPHO prepares.
- MPHO coordinates.

Do not assign overlapping meanings.

---

## 12. Naming rules for new modules

A new branded module should only be created when:

1. It has a distinct audience or function.
2. The function is stable.
3. The name adds clarity.
4. It does not duplicate an existing module.
5. The product owner approves it.
6. Documentation is updated.

Prefer functional labels over unnecessary sub-brands.

Example:

Use `Seguimiento del pedido` instead of inventing a new brand for tracking.

## 14. Customer copy principles

### Be clear

Use:

> Entrega estimada entre 5:00 y 7:00 p. m.

Avoid:

> Llegará muy pronto.

### Be honest

Use:

> Este producto requiere llegar primero al Punto MPHO.

Avoid:

> Disponible hoy.

### Explain cost

Use:

- Regalo.
- Personalización.
- Entrega.
- Servicio MPHO.

Avoid hiding all charges inside one unexplained price when a breakdown is operationally relevant.

### Reduce anxiety

Use:

> El aliado ya recibió tu pedido.

Avoid:

> Procesando.

### Show next step

Use:

> Estamos esperando que el producto llegue al Punto MPHO.

Avoid:

> En curso.

## 15. Partner copy principles

Partner language should be:

- Operational.
- Short.
- Actionable.
- Specific.

Preferred order card:

```text
Pedido MPHO-1042
Aceptar antes de: 3:15 p. m.
Preparar antes de: 5:00 p. m.
Ganancia estimada: visible after acceptance rules
```

Preferred actions:

- Aceptar.
- Rechazar.
- Confirmar existencia.
- Producto recibido.
- Reportar daño.
- Iniciar preparación.
- Subir evidencia.
- Marcar listo.
- Entregado al repartidor.

Avoid vague labels:

- Continuar.
- Procesar.
- Hecho.
- Siguiente.

## 16. HADIA voice

HADIA should:

- Ask one useful question at a time when possible.
- Explain recommendations.
- Respect budget.
- Avoid pressure.
- Offer alternatives.
- Mention uncertainty.
- Confirm important delivery details.

Example:

> Para recomendarte opciones reales, necesito saber la zona de entrega y la fecha.

HADIA should not say:

> Te garantizo que llegará hoy.

Unless eligibility and guarantee rules explicitly allow it.

---

## 17. MPHORA voice

MPHORA copy should communicate speed with conditions.

Preferred:

> Disponible para entrega rápida en esta zona.

Preferred:

> Compra antes de las 4:00 p. m. para conservar esta opción.

Avoid:

> Entrega inmediata.

Avoid:

> En una hora garantizado.

Unless a later approved service level supports it.

## 18. Visual identity direction

The final visual system will be defined in `28_DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`.

Current strategic direction:

- Premium but accessible.
- Warm rather than cold.
- Modern digital platform.
- Strong mobile readability.
- Clear status colors.
- Distinct visual treatment for HADIA and MPHORA without separating them from MPHO.
- Photography focused on real gifts, local preparation, hands, details, and delivery moments.
- Avoid generic marketplace clutter.

No final colors, logos, fonts, or icon systems are approved by this document.

Potential logo system:

- Punto MPHO using a partner badge.

## 20. Brand trust rules

Never use brand language to hide:

- External-product uncertainty.
- Delivery restrictions.
- Extra fees.
- Partner limitations.
- Cancellation rules.
- Refund conditions.
- Product substitutions.
- Missing evidence.

Trust is a product feature.

## 22. Prohibited brand usage

Do not:

- Present HADIA as a separate company.
- Use MPHORA for unverified availability.
- Call every shop a Punto MPHO.
- Create logos for future modules before approval.
- Use several slogans simultaneously on the same screen.
- Mix English and Spanish randomly in customer-facing copy.
- Use the brand to imply legal or logistical guarantees that do not exist.
- Use partner photos without authorization.
- Use external marketplace logos in a way that suggests official partnership without confirmation.
